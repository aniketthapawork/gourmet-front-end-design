Our Story

A journey of culinary passion, innovation, and the pursuit of gastronomic excellence

The Birth of Lumière

Founded in 2015 by renowned chef Antoine Dubois, Lumière was born from a vision to create an extraordinary dining experience that would illuminate the senses. Our name, meaning "light" in French, represents our commitment to bringing brightness and innovation to the culinary world.

What started as a dream in a small Parisian kitchen has evolved into a destination for food enthusiasts from around the globe. Every dish tells a story, every ingredient is carefully sourced, and every meal is crafted with the passion that defines our culinary philosophy.

Today, Lumière stands as a beacon of gastronomic excellence, where tradition meets innovation, and where every guest becomes part of our continuing story.

Meet Our Executive Chef

The visionary behind our culinary masterpieces

Chef Antoine Dubois

Executive Chef & Founder

With over 20 years of culinary excellence, Chef Antoine Dubois brings a wealth of experience from some of the world's most prestigious kitchens. Trained at Le Cordon Bleu Paris and having worked alongside Michelin-starred chefs across Europe, Antoine's philosophy centers on respecting ingredients while pushing creative boundaries.

His innovative approach combines classical French techniques with modern presentation and global influences, creating dishes that are both visually stunning and explosively flavorful.

Awards: James Beard Rising Chef Award 2018
Recognition: Michelin Star 2019, 2020, 2021
Featured: Food & Wine "Best Chefs in America"
